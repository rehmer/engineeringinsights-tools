About This Advanced Calculator

Bidirectional Flow Conversion:

  • Volumetric → Mass: Input ACFM, get mass flow rates
  • Mass → Volumetric: Input mass flow, get ACFM/SCFM
  • Real Gas Accuracy: Uses interpolated CoolProp density data

Graham's Law Applications:

  • Leak Testing: Convert from helium leak rates to actual gas rates
  • Process Scaling: Scale flow rates when changing process gases
  • Gas Substitution: Find equivalent flow rates for different gases
  • Calibration: Convert between test gas and measurement gas

When to Use Graham's Law:

  • Effusion through porous media (leak testing, membranes)
  • Molecular flow conditions (low pressure, small orifices)
  • Gas chromatography scaling and method development
  • Mass spectrometer leak detector conversions
Note: Graham's Law applies to molecular effusion and diffusion. For bulk flow through pipes or large orifices, use standard flow scaling based on density and pressure differences.
